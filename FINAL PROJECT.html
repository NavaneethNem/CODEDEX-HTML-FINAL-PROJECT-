Shoyu RamenRamen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish originating from Chinese cuisine, consisting of wheat noodles in a flavorful broth, often topped with ingredients like meat, seaweed, and green onions.

2.Chopsuey

Chop suey is a Chinese-American and overseas Chinese dish of stir-fried meat, eggs, and vegetables, often including bean sprouts, cabbage, and celery, bound in a starch-thickened sauce.

3.Triple Rice

"Triple rice," more commonly known as Triple Schezwan Rice, refers to an Indo-Chinese dish that combines rice, noodles, and a spicy Schezwan gravy as its three main components, forming a hearty, complete meal.
